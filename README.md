# Pandas-Challenge

This Challenge consists of two data CSV files that contain data on schools and students. In the file 'PyCitySchools.ipynb' you will see the analysis of the merged two CSV files.

What was done:

District Summary
  Total schools
  Total students
  Total budget
  Average math score
  Average reading score
  % passing math (the percentage of students who passed math)
  % passing reading (the percentage of students who passed reading)
  % overall passing (the percentage of students who passed math AND reading)

School Summary
  School name
  School type
  Total students
  Total school budget
  Per student budget
  Average math score
  Average reading score
  % passing math (the percentage of students who passed math)
  % passing reading (the percentage of students who passed reading)
  % overall passing (the percentage of students who passed math AND reading)
